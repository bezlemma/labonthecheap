A DIY CO2 Incubator for just $350

March 26, 2018

Growing cells?  Need an incubator or a bioreactor? These things generally  cost upwards of five grand, but here Andrew Pelling's lab has made a DIY CO2 reactor for around $350. It might look a bit janky, but it's a legit piece of equipment when you get into it.

The website includes detailed instructions, cost analysis, and best of all proof of results.
