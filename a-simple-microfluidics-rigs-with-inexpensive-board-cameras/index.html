A Simple Microfluidics Rigs with Inexpensive Board Cameras

May 1, 2018

An anonymous reader emailed us to share a small DIY mi­croflu­idic rig equipped with inexpensive board cameras. The chassis is mainly made out of laser-­cut acrylic sheets, M6 nuts, and bolts.  This GitHub page gives a comprehensive list of components complete with their cost, and instructions on how to assemble them.
