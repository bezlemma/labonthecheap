UV Lamps

March 13, 2018

Need a UV lamp for curing purposes? Consider buying a commercial product for curing nail polish. Amazon has a whole category just for UV Nail Dryers !

Some cardboard and tinfoil does a good job of isolating UV rays, and a nice hinge can be made with Velcro.
